The landing page and central hub for navigating the site.

Projects

A showcase of my work and ongoing developments.

My personal insights and learnings, organized by topic. These are processed thoughts and summaries from various materials I've studied.

A curated list of books, podcasts, articles, and other materials I've found valuable or plan to explore.

- This is a place to host my learning and my collection of learning resources. - Goal is to have an extremely high rate of learning. Enough to get me to the top 1% of learners. My interests are in the game development, game design, and indie game business areas. - Project based learning is under projects, to try and create things from my learning. - The notes section is for a mind-map like series of notes on various resources. goal is to NOT summarize and regurgitate, but to create my own genuine understanding in a “knowledge tree” (concepts are branches, details are leaves). searching for how the information connects to one another. - Problems is a section for placing the most important, absolutely most vital “what do i need to solves” in my life. not sure if i will keep this public. - resources is a collection of high quality material that i can refer back to later, with the intention of taking detailed notes on all of them. this is essentially the “queue”. ideally i'll be taking one of these resources per week and breaking it down into a mind map and putting it in the notes section.
